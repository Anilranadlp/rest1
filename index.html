Our Story

Discover the legacy behind India's most trusted hospitality brand

Founded in 2015 with a vision to redefine hospitality in India, Rester Hotels has grown from a single property to a nationally recognized brand with multiple awards for excellence in service and guest satisfaction.

Our philosophy centers around creating spaces that feel like home while delivering service that anticipates every need. We combine contemporary design with traditional Indian hospitality values to create memorable experiences.

With properties in Jodhpur, Chakan Pune, Itarasi, and Indore, and three new developments underway, we're committed to expanding our footprint while maintaining the highest standards of quality and service that our guests expect.

Every Rester Hotels property reflects our core values of integrity, excellence, and genuine care for our guests, making us the preferred choice for business and leisure travelers alike.
